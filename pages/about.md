# About

![Jeff Street]({{ '/assets/images/About.jpg' | relative_url }}){: .about-photo}

I'm a technical product leader with more than 15 years of experience leading small teams to develop hardware/software systems at the forefront of innovation.

## Approach

I build things. I lead small teams through complete product development cycles -- from napkin sketch to production. I'm most effective when I can stay close to the technical work while also owning product direction.

## Background

My career spans drones, aerospace, robotics, and medical devices. I've designed biomimetic submarines, launched industrial drone platforms, published peer-reviewed research on medical drone transport, and held patents for UAV cargo systems. I started in physics and mechanical engineering, which gives me a strong foundation for working across disciplines.

## Perspective

I believe the best products come from teams where everyone builds. I've worked at startups where I was the first technical hire, at research institutions where I founded new programs, and at established companies where I launched flagship products. In every case, the pattern is the same: small teams, clear ownership, fast iteration.

## Direction

I'm currently focused on drone delivery systems at [Fly ZOOZ](https://flyzooz.com), where I'm the founding product leader. I'm interested in the intersection of autonomous systems, logistics, and hardware that works in the real world.
