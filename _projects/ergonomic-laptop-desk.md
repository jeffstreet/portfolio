We are renting a furnished apartment, which came with an IKEA [BESTÅ BURS](http://www.ikea.com/us/en/catalog/products/70245339/) desk. To make the workspace more ergonomic, I added an [EKBY TONY](http://www.ikea.com/us/en/catalog/products/S79048179/) shelf, supported by [EKBY TÖRE](http://www.ikea.com/us/en/catalog/products/10168720/#/50251029) clamp-on shelf risers.
