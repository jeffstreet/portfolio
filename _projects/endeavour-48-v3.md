Created a better strategy for using the [full-scale lines](http://jeffstreet.com/post/103803587147/endeavour48-full-scale-lines) as guides. Made many copies and erased all but the appropriate section lines. Laid them out to align with the section.

Found the lines to be inconsistent. For example, vertical and horizontal sections do not intersect. Some artistic liberty was needed.

Attempted to create the hull surface via freeform surface. Results were better than the lofting method used in [Version 2](http://jeffstreet.com/post/103804155052/endeavour48-v2), however there are still a few trouble areas.
