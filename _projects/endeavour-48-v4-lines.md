For my Dad’s Christmas present, I made this drawing of the 48 inch scale lines and had it printed it on a 36 x 60 inch piece of paper.

These lines were generated by making cross-sections in a SolidWorks Drawing. The drawing was exported as an Adobe Illustrator file, which I used to align the sections and add notations.

Because these cross-sections lines are derived directly from the SolidWorks model, any future changes to the model will be automatically reflected in the SolidWorks drawing. Unfortunately, the final alignment and notation steps will have to be repeated manually because they were performed in Illustrator.

**[Download PDF](https://www.dropbox.com/s/66u653cu9h6r6kc/2013-12-15%20-%20Endeavour%2048%20V4%20-%20Lines.pdf?dl=0)**

To print this PDF, I’d recommend working with a blueprint shop. They’re used to printing on large sheets, and the prints are inexpensive (these drawings cost $3 per sheet, black and white).
