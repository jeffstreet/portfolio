**[MedicalDrones.org](http://MedicalDrones.org)**

Medical Drones are small unmanned aircraft that transport medical supplies between remote communities and well-equipped medical facilities. We are designing aircraft that can safely transport medical cargos, and will shortly be publishing our first research paper.
