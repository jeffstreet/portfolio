Aircraft developed for a study of the effects of drone flight on microorganisms. Interfaced with medical personnel to distill the aircraft and specimen requirements, selected a suitable airframe, and designed modifications. Flights with live specimens performed in partnership with Johns Hopkins using human blood and sputum seeded with microorganisms. CNN Vital Signs with Dr. Sanjay Gupta featured this study in March 2016.
