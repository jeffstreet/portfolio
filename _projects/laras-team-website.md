My sister, Lara, passed away in her twenties from brain cancer. My mother honors her memory by working with the [Seattle Brain Cancer Walk](http://www.braincancerwalk.org) and leading a fundraising team herself: Lara’s Team. The team needed a website, so I taught her how to use Tumblr and we built [laras-team.org](http://laras-team.org).
