Aircraft developed for a study of the effects of drone flight on human blood chemistry samples. Interfaced with medical personnel, selected airframe, designed modifications. Included iterative design, crash testing, compliance with IATA medical transport laws, and performance evaluation flights. Flights performed in partnership with Johns Hopkins. [Published by PLOS One in July 2015.](http://journals.plos.org/plosone/article?id=10.1371/journal.pone.0134020)
