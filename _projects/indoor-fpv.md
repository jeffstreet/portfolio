The Inductrix is a palm-size quadcopter that can carry a tiny camera with built-in video transmitter. FliteTest.com made a [video about using them together](http://flitetest.com/articles/diy-micro-fpv-race-quad) for micro-scale FPV flying.

Here’s everything you need:

- [Inductrix with radio (RTF)](http://store.flitetest.com/inductrix-rtf-with-safe-technology/): $70
- [Inductrix batteries (Qty. 4)](https://amzn.com/B00QSP76YY): $18
- [Camera / Video transmitter](http://store.flitetest.com/hyperion-mini-600-tvl-camera-w-built-in-5-8ghz-25mw-vtx/): $55
- [Monitor / Video receiver](http://store.flitetest.com/eachine-5-8g-40ch-7-diversity-fpv-monitor-w-dvr/): $130
- Optional: [4-port battery charger](https://amzn.com/B001XIMGP0): $30 and [Power supply](https://amzn.com/B001XIMGR8): $17
