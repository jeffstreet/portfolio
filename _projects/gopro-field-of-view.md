Everybody knows what GoPro video looks like. It’s got a very distinctive fisheye feel. While that’s a useful perspective while flying a drone, we’re not accustomed to seeing much fisheye on the movie screen. By narrowing the GoPro’s field of view, the output can be made to look much more cinematic.

Here’s a sample shot with the GoPro 3+ Black, which has [several field of view settings](http://vimeo.com/91540615): 14mm, 21mm, 28mm. The video above is shot at 28mm, the “Narrow” field of view.
