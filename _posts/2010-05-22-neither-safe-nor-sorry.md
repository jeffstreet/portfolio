After a trackday at Chuckwalla, I ran out of gas on the way back to town. Yes, they had gas at the track, but I was too cheap to buy an insurance splash at the end of the day. AAA said they would need an hour to rescue me, so I passed the time by changing my brake pads under an overpass. Just barely made it back to the hotel before dark.
