I got this paddleboard because I like cycling and wanted an upper-body workout that is a similar experience. SUP paddling is a great exercise, and this board is perfect for long paddles because it has a displacement hull, which makes it more efficient, and it’s wide enough to be stable even on choppy days. I launch it from Torrance beach and it punches right through the waves.

It’s got GoPro mounts, top and bottom. There are tie-downs in the front and back. They work great for a water bottle or camera or a lunch bag. And they’re perfect for carrying your paddle to the beach.

The paddle is really long. I’m 6'1", but no worries if you’re not as tall. Just take it over to Jeremy at Olympus Board Shop on Torrance Blvd, and he can cut it down for you.

Details

- The board is a [Riviera Voyager 12’ 6"](http://www.rivierapaddlesurf.com/collections/riviera/products/riviera-126-voyager-stand-up-paddle-board-w-handle-traction) ($1,100 new)
- The paddle is a [Riviera ASSET 8.5, Danny Ching model](http://www.rivierapaddlesurf.com/collections/sup-paddles/products/riviera-asset-carbon-fiber-sup-paddle), comes with cover ($254 new)
- The PFD is a [Stearns SOSpender](http://amzn.com/B004D344F2) (groan) ($115 new)
